CTL: Linux Ansible Controller

DC: Domain Controller

Identity Flow

Ansible requests tickets from KDC. Requires 88, 53, 123 to be open from Controller to DC.

Hardening Flow

Targets pull GPO and Certs. Requires 389, 445, 135, 443 from Targets to DC.

Management Flow

Encrypted automation commands. Requires 5986 from Controller to all Targets.
